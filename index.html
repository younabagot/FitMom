Welcome to Fit'mom

Fit'Moms is a fitness club exclusively for mothers, to provide them not only with private training sessions but also kindergarten services for their children.

About Us

We created the "Fit' Moms" concept because we noticed how difficult it is for new mothers and mothers in general to work out in a gym because of a lack of time for themselves. They either have to go to work, look after their home and children. There is also the lack of motivation, the inability to leave its child to go to train. Going to the gym for a mother becomes a logistical and therefore a difficult task, which is why many mothers just decide to give up. However, we know that after a birth, a woman's body changes and takes some time to return to normal. During this period, these women may lack confidence and self-assurance. This is why we created Fit' Moms, an innovative concept that allows mothersto train and get back to their dream body without having to worry about the safety of their children as they will be taken care of babysitters and they will not feel judged because they will be in a safe place surrounded by mothersthat can understand them. Therefore, Fit moms is a fitness club exclusively for mothers, to provide them not only with private training sessions but also kindergarten servicesfortheir children.

Our Classes

Pilates

Strengthen your core and improve flexibility with our Pilates classes.

One of the best ways to achieve a strong and flexible body is through Pilates. Our Pilates classes are specifically designed to meet the unique needs of moms. By focusing on your core muscles, you'll develop a strong foundation that supports everything you do - from carrying groceries to chasing after your little ones.

But Pilates isn't just about strength. It's also about improving flexibility, balance, and posture - all of which are important for preventing injuries and reducing stress on your body. As a result, you'll feel more comfortable and confident in your everyday movements.

Yoga

Relax your mind and body with our yoga classes.

Our yoga classes are designed specifically for moms who want to relax their minds and bodies. Our experienced instructors will guide you through each pose, helping you to release tension and find inner peace. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced yogi, our classes are perfect for you.

Yoga is not only great for improving flexibility and strength, but it's also an amazing way to reduce stress and anxiety. With our yoga classes, you'll be able to escape the daily grind and find a sense of calmness and serenity.

Zumba

Burn calories and have fun with our Zumba classes.

Our Zumba classes are designed specifically for moms, so you can feel comfortable and supported every step of the way. Our experienced instructors will guide you through each move, helping you get the most out of your workout while having fun and meeting other moms just like you.

With our Zumba classes, you'll dance your way to a healthier, happier you. You'll get your heart pumping, sweat off those extra calories, and boost your energy levels all at once. And with classes scheduled at convenient times throughout the day, you'll be able to fit your workout into your busy schedule.
